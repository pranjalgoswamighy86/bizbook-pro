Double-Entry Accounting

Full General Ledger with journal entries, trial balance, P&L, and balance sheet. Every transaction follows Assets = Liabilities + Equity.

  • Debtors (AR) & Creditors (AP) sub-ledgers
  • GST split (CGST/SGST/IGST)
  • Chart of Accounts (26 standard accounts)
  • Trial Balance with balance verification

Sales & Purchase

  • E-invoice integration (IRN/AckNo)
  • Excel export with Payment Mode column
